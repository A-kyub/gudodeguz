ANTI-ANIMAL ABUSE

written by: Jan Angel De Guzman 


A lot of people nowadays would always like to buy animals as pet in their house. At first, they would treat them well, but as time pass by they would get tired of taking care of these pets and eventually treat them as pests. Most of us had seen animals being abused phisically or verbally. For example are the street dogs and cats who are getting hit by someone, the birds that are caught and is sold in the market, pets that are not being fed right, and the destruction of the home or habitat of the wild animals. These are just some of the inhumane act that we often see. Not minding these things are some of our characteristics, thinking that it would be troublesome to meddle into someone else's business. But even though they did'nt listen, we should atleast warn them. If they still did it a few times despite the warning, we must tell what are they doing to the authority. Another way to prevent these things from happening, acting responsible is the best answer. These animals would like someone to accept them. They are also living beings like humans who would want to be loved in return.
